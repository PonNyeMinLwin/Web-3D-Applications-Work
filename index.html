Monster Beverage Corporation

Invented by Hubert Hansen

In 1935, the company was founded under the name of 'Hansen Natural Corporation' by Hubert Hansen in order to sell juice products. However, in 2002, the company rebranded itself as Monster Beverage Corporation and began focusing on developing and selling energy drinks, gaining rapid popularity for its wide range of flavors and unique branding. As of 2023, the Monster Beverage Corporation is the second largest manufacturer and distributor of energy drinks in the United States, owning a significant 30% of the U.S energy drink market share.


Monster Energy: Original

The original Monster Energy carbonated energy drink was first introduced in 2002. The recipe was developed by Hubert Hansen, who was inspired by the need for a high-energy beverage that could provide a quick boost of energy. The drink was primarly marketed towards athletes, students, and individuals with active or adventurous lifestyles as it contained a blend of caffeine, taurine, B vitamins, and other energy-inducing ingredients. Due to the immense success of this drink, the distinctive black and green can, with the iconic Monster Energy claw logo became the company's universally recognized symbol.

Monster Energy: Ultra Rosá

In 2012, Monster Energy introduced the "Ultra" line - a new low-calorie sugar-free alternative to the original Monster Energy drinks. The variant "Ultra Rosá", released in 2020, was concocted with a blend of strawberry and watermelon, giving it "a crisp and complex flavour with a floral finish". This drink became a popular choice among consumers due to its lighter, less-carbonated taste that offered a unique, refreshing alternative to other general energy drinks, while providing the same energy boost.

Monster Energy: The Doctor

Monster Energy: The Doctor was a limited edition variant, made with a blend of citrus and tropical flavours, released to celebrate Monster's partnership with MotoGP and to commemorate the legendary motorcycle racer, Valentino Rossi, who is often referred to as "The Doctor". This drink was inspired by Rossi's iconic number and brand VR46 and was held and displayed whenever Rossi goes on the podium. By parterning with MotoGP and Valentino Rossi, Monster Energy builds their brand, cementing their image as a high-performance energy drink that is associated with success, andrenaline, and adventure.
