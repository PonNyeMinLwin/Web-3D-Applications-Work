The Original Green Monster Energy

Flavour Profile: Fizzy, Sweet, Salty

The original Monster Energy carbonated energy drink was first introduced in 2002. The recipe was developed by Hubert Hansen, who was inspired by the need for a high-energy beverage that could provide a quick boost of energy. The drink was primarly marketed towards athletes, students, and individuals with active or adventurous lifestyles as it contained a blend of caffeine, taurine, B vitamins, and other energy-inducing ingredients. Due to the immense success of this drink, the distinctive black and green can, with the iconic Monster Energy claw logo became the company's universally recognized symbol.
