VR 46 AKA The Doctor Monster Energy

Flavour Profile: Light, Crisp, Tropical, Refreshing Citrus

Monster Energy: The Doctor was a limited edition variant, made with a blend of citrus and tropical flavours, released to celebrate Monster's partnership with MotoGP and to commemorate the legendary motorcycle racer, Valentino Rossi, who is often referred to as "The Doctor". This drink was inspired by Rossi's iconic number and brand VR46 and was held and displayed whenever Rossi goes on the podium. By parterning with MotoGP and Valentino Rossi, Monster Energy builds their brand, cementing their image as a high-performance energy drink that is associated with success, andrenaline, and adventure.
